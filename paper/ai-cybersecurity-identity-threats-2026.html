AI Cybersecurity Identity Threats in 2026

Saksham Shekher
Ganpat University · MCA (Cybersecurity)
Published: January 30, 2026

Abstract

Artificial intelligence has become a defining force in modern cybersecurity systems, influencing identity management, threat detection, and automated defense mechanisms. This paper presents a theoretical analysis of AI-enabled identity threats, including synthetic identity impersonation, adaptive social engineering, and autonomous exploitation.
